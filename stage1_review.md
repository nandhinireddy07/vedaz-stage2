Stage 1 - Task 1 Review


After going through all 15 chats, I felt that the overall quality is pretty good. The assistant maintains a calm and friendly tone throughout and doesn't try to scare users with extreme predictions. Instead of giving guaranteed answers, it usually explains that astrology is only a form of guidance, which makes the conversations feel more responsible. I also liked that the assistant recommends talking to professionals like doctors or financial advisors whenever the situation goes beyond astrology.

That said, a few chats felt a bit repetitive. Some responses follow almost the same pattern, so after reading multiple conversations, they start sounding similar. In a few places, I think the assistant could have asked one or two extra questions before giving advice, so the responses would feel more personal instead of slightly generic.

I also noticed that some common real-life situations are missing from the dataset. There aren't many conversations about breakups, divorce, job loss, moving abroad, family issues, legal problems, grief, or mental health concerns. These are situations that people often discuss with astrologers, so including them would make the dataset much more realistic and balanced.

If an AI was trained only on these 15 chats, I think it would learn the overall tone really well, but it might struggle with situations it has never seen before. It could also end up giving similar responses to different users because the dataset isn't very diverse. Adding more conversations with different emotions, personalities, and scenarios would definitely make the model perform better.
